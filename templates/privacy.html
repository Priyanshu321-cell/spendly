Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects only the data you voluntarily provide: expense records (amount, category, date, description), account information for registration, and usage data to improve our service.

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. You retain full control over your data and can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
